
1. Bowl Cut Is Back: Embrace Your Rebellious Nature with Style.

A vintage cut has evolved into a versatile fashion-forward hairstyle that can be matched with your personality. Forget about awkward school photos and dive into the endless range of statement-worthy possibilities!.

2. Curtains Hairstyles.

We bet with this mid length hair men style, you will be considered the trendiest guy in the area. A curtain bang is at the peak of its popularity at the moment. So, if you try to keep up with fashion, this is your sure bet. While it works better on straight hair, you can make it work for any hair type.

3. Wavy Haircut.

The waves have an innate ability to reinvent any structure, and a bowl cut would be an exception. A style like that would add more movement to the hair, which in turn results in a relaxed and refined look.

4. Layered Cut.

Layers are often a synonym of the dynamics your mane can acquire when styled appropriately. In this variation, a bowl cut looks both deep and soft, though strategic. Sometimes, it takes as much as a casual run through the locks to style the hair impeccably and expressively.

5. Textured Mohawk Fade

Do you feel like you are ready to pull off that rock star vibe? Then a mohawk is precisely one of those medium length mens haircuts you require. No need to opt for all the colorful drama of 70's punks. You can act classy and daring at the same time with modern mohawks.

6. Spiky Bowl.

Care to reinterpret the classics? We've got an idea! A spiky bowl cut vibrates with raw punk energy but maintains the classy lines for a sharp outline. It does not take long to style the look due to strategic layering, although we recommend that you invest in a strong-hold styling product to keep the shape exquisite and distinguishable.
